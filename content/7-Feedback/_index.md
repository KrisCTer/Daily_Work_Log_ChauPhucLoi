### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment:**  
The working environment is professional, friendly, and open. The colleagues in the company are always willing to help when I encounter difficulties, creating a comfortable atmosphere for learning and exchanging ideas. The workspace is neat, providing good conditions for concentration during the internship.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin:**  
The mentor provides dedicated guidance, explains things clearly, and always creates opportunities for me to proactively learn and solve problems on my own before stepping in to help. The team admin quickly assists with procedures and documents, and is always ready to answer questions when needed, helping the internship process go smoothly.

**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major:**  
The assigned tasks are aligned with my major, while also allowing me to access many new technologies and real-world workflows. This helps me reinforce the knowledge I have learned and further develop my professional skills.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities:**  
Interning at the company provides many opportunities to learn about technical aspects as well as working skills in a corporate environment. I was exposed to project development processes, teamwork, task management, and improved my self-learning and problem-solving abilities.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit:**  
The work culture is positive; everyone respects and supports each other. Team members are willing to share experiences and coordinate effectively, helping me quickly integrate and motivating me to develop myself.

**6. Internship Policies / Benefits:**  
The company creates favorable conditions for interns through training programs, learning materials, and mentor support. The internship policy is reasonable, providing interns with an environment to learn and accumulate practical experience.


### Additional Questions

**What did you find most satisfying during your internship?**  
What I was most satisfied with was participating in real tasks and receiving dedicated guidance from my mentor, as well as support from the team members. The open working environment helped me become more confident in learning and developing my skills.

**What do you think the company should improve for future interns?**  
The company could build a more detailed internship roadmap for each stage, along with specific goals so that interns can easily track their learning and development progress. In addition, having more experience-sharing sessions between teams or technical workshops would help interns learn much more.

**If recommending to a friend, would you suggest they intern here? Why or why not?**  
Yes. I would recommend it because the company has a professional working environment, dedicated mentors, many learning opportunities, and allows participation in real projects. This is a suitable environment for students to accumulate experience and develop skills before starting official employment.


### Suggestions & Expectations

**Expectations for the company:**  
I hope the company continues to maintain the current learning environment and adds more in-depth training sessions, as well as experience sharing from senior professionals. At the same time, organizing more networking activities could enhance the connection among members.

**Would you like to continue this program in the future?**  
Yes. I wish to continue accompanying the company if given the opportunity to learn more, improve my expertise, and contribute more to real projects.

**Other comments:**  
Thank you to the company, mentor, and team members for always supporting and creating conditions for me to have a meaningful internship. This period has helped me mature in both professional knowledge and working skills. I hope the company continues to grow and bring many learning opportunities to future generations of interns.
